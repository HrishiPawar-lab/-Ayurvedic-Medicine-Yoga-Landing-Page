    Ayurved

    Ayurveda means Science of life. It recommends methods for appropriate living and longevity. It includes maintaining good health as well as dealing with illness through Yoga, treatments, herbal medicines and lifestyle changes

    Panchkarma

    Panchakarma is an Ayurvedic therapeutic approach for the body, mind, and consciousness that detoxifies and rejuvenates.

    Yog

    Yoga therapy is a mind-body practice that focuses on your physical, emotional and mental health. It uses yoga postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve mental and physical health.

    Bone Setting

    Traditional bone setting is a natural therapy/manual therapy to rebalance our body and bone structure. It helps improve joint mobility, alleviate pain, and promote healing.

    Cupping

    Cupping is an alternative therapy that involves suctioning the skin with glass, plastic or silicone cups. This therapy is used to stimulate blood flow, loosen muscle tension, and promote healing.

    Naturopathy

    Naturopathy favors a holistic approach with non-invasive treatment and generally avoids the use of surgery and drugs to heal the body inside out naturally.
